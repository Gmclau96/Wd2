WD2 Resteraunt opened in 1967 and is a family owned business. We specialise in using only the freshest ingredients to create high quality meals.

All chefs at WD2 have completed chef school and come out with distinctions, so you know that all food prepared and serverd at our resteraunt will look and taste fantastic.

As we are commited to using fresh ingredients, please be sure to check our menu everyday as it changes daily due to the ingredients available. We also rotate through our specials, so make sure to keep an eye out when your favourite is on the menu!

All at WD2 have a zero tolerancy policy againt any wasted food, and at the end of every night, our chefs will make dishes with the ingredients we have to spare and donate these to local food banks.

Lunches are served from opening until 4pm everyday, while the dinner menu is available from 4pm onwards. The sides, desserts and drinks are available all day.
